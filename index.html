About Us

Welcome to JJ's Roti Delight, a culinary gem where the vibrant flavors of the Caribbean come alive. We are passionate about bringing you authentic roti and a delightful array of traditional dishes, meticulously prepared with the freshest ingredients and a touch of homemade love. Nestled in the heart of Port Union, Toronto, our restaurant offers a warm and inviting atmosphere, perfect for a quick, flavorful lunch, a relaxed family dinner, or a lively gathering with friends. Come experience the taste of the islands right here at JJ's Roti Delight, your go-to destination for an unforgettable Caribbean culinary journey.
